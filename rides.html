Universal Studios Premier Adventure Park

Park Rides & Attractions

Explore exciting rides below.

The Incredible Hulk Coaster

The Incredible Hulk Coaster is a gamma radiation themed coaster famously known for shooting riders from 0 to 40 mph in 2 seconds uphill into a zero-g roll. Reaching speeds of 67 mph.

KrustyLand

Krustyland is a themed area centered around motion simulation. Experience the Simpson family through chaotic attractions in 4D.

VelociCoaster

The VelociCoaster is a high-speed intense ride reaching speeds of 70 mph in 2.4 seconds. It is the tallest and fastest launch coaster in florida.

Transformers Ride

The Transformers come to life in this 4D motion simulator that puts you inside a battle betweeen Autobots and Decepticons.

Rip Ride Rockit

This is a 167-foot tall, music-themed steel coaster reaching 65 mph. It also features a unique 90-degree lift hill and onboard audio that lets a user pick their own music.
